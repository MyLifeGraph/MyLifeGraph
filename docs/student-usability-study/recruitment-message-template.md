# Recruitment Message Template

Replace or remove every bracketed field before sending. Mention compensation
only when it has actually been approved.

## Short invitation

> We are testing an English-language student planning app and are looking for
> current students aged 18 or older who plan coursework, exams, or assignments.
> The study has a 35–45 minute think-aloud session and a 10–15 minute follow-up
> after one planned study block. We test the product, not you. Please use
> fictional study and wellbeing details; we do not need private academic or
> health information. Participation is voluntary and you may stop at any time.
> The session is [in person / remote with screen sharing] on [dates].
> [Compensation, if real.] Interested?

## Screening questions

Ask only what is needed for eligibility and variation:

1. Are you currently enrolled as a student and at least 18 years old?
2. Do you currently plan coursework, an exam, or an assignment?
3. Are you comfortable navigating an app whose V1 interface is in English?
4. Can you attend the first session and a short follow-up after a scheduled
   preparation block?
5. How confident do you feel planning study work today: `1 — not confident` to
   `5 — very confident`?
6. Which broad description fits best: early degree, later degree, postgraduate,
   vocational, or another current-student context?

Do not request grades, diagnoses, legal names for the study artifact, exact
course titles, or private calendar contents. Store contact and scheduling data
outside the repository. Eligibility is not consent; obtain note and optional
capture consent again at the session.
