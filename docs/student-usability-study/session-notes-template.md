## Outcome scale

- `completed`: finished without product help.
- `completed with hesitation`: finished without product help after a visible
  pause, wrong turn, or backtrack.
- `blocked`: required intervention level 3 or could not finish.
- `not observed`: the task was not reached or Part B did not happen.

Intervention: `0 none`, `1 neutral`, `2 scenario clarification`,
`3 product help`. SEQ: `1 very difficult` through `7 very easy`.
